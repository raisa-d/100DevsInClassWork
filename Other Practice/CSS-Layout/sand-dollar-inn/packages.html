Packages

The Sand Dollar Inn offers six breathtaking packages to help you fully experience life on Sulani.

Sulani Adventures

  • Guided tour of Mua Pel'am to explore the lush landscapes and hidden gems of Sulani.
  • Snorkeling and scuba diving equipment Rental for an aquatic adventure.
  • Sunset cruise with sweeping views of the ocean and islands of Sulani.

Mermaid Retreat

  • Exclusive mermaid encounter experience with a local guide.
  • Mermaid inspired spa treatments, including seaweed wraps and oceanic facials.
  • Complimentary mermaid tail rental for a day of swimming in the clear blue waters.
    Maybe even find mermadic kelp and pop a real tail!

Volcano Serenity

  • Relaxing spa day with volcanic stone massages and tropical aromatherapy.
  • Private sunset yoga session with a view of the majestic Mua Pel'
  • Volcano-themed room decor and amenities for an immersive experience.

Couples Paradise

  • Romantic beachfront dinner under the stars.
  • Couples massage and spa day in a private cabana.
  • Sunset sailing excursion with champagne and gorgeous views.

Island Explorer

  • Access to a personalized island exploration map with recommended sights.
  • Complimentary rental of bikes, kayaks, and other adventure equipment.
  • Picnic lunch provided for a day of exploring the beauty of Sulani.

Family Fun Escape

  • Kid-friendly activities, including sandcastle building contests and treasure hunts.
  • Family-friendly movie nights with popcorn and treats.
  • Ice cream social to meet the other kids.
